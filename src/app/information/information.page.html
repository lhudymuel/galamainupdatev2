GALA

In the Philippine province of Batangas, there is a small municipality known as Mabini. It is known for its agricultural industry, scenic beauty, and rich cultural legacy. With attractions such as beaches, resorts, and historical sites to explore, Gala is a tour assistant application that helps not only the tourist but also the people of Mabini, with the help of the application user can easily navigate different tourist spot like beaches, coffee shops, stores and more. In addition, every category has a provided map which can help the user to easily locate the selected resorts, shops, cafe, restaurant and more.
